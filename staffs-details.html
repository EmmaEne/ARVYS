Teaching Philosophy

I spend most of my time researching how to make learning to code fun and make hard concepts easy to understand. Expect lots of projects, explanations, and a touch of geeky humour.

About

I'm Angela, a developer with a passion for teaching. I'm the lead instructor at the London App Brewery, London's leading Programming Bootcamp. I've helped hundreds of thousands of students learn to code and change their lives by becoming developers.

My first foray into programming was at age 12 when I wanted to build my own Space Invader game. Since then, I've made hundreds of websites, apps, and games — but my greatest passion is teaching.

I apply everything I learn about effective teaching to my courses — from humour to hands-on projects — making complex concepts simple and fun.
